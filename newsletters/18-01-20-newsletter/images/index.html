Back like we never left


Hi everyone and happy 2020!! Hopefully you have had a great winter break and have aced all your January exams!

As you may know, the studio has known some issues throughout December, which have made it unusable during the whole month. I am glad to tell you that all the necessary works have been carried out and that the studio is now as fresh as ever! Finally, some great recording opportunities are happening in the next two months. If you want to be a part of it, please email icrproduction@ic.ac.uk.
