More about the project

By mixing genres and languages, the duo GioLeo aims to create songs that leave a distinct aftertaste. Their debut single GAMMA showcases the writing skills of Gio as he intertwines English and Mandarin rap while the methodic production of Leo shines on the jumpy club bangers Snake and Bomb (released 3 November).
