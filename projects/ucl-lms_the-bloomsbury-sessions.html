More about the project

Released on the 1st September 2019, 'The Bloomsbury Sessions' are available on all streaming platforms for the first time in their history.

The album features seven original tracks from some of the society's most exciting writers, recorded over two days in May and early June. This year's sessions have been a labour of love for all involved. From Alternative Rock to Cinematic Pop, Jazz Fusion to intimate Acoustic Performance, the album will certainly have a little something for everybody to enjoy.

The seventh track, from the winners of UCL's 2018/2019 Battle of the Bands, Bandra Hursel, is accompanied by a live video shot in the ICR Production live room.
